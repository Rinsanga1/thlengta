# Thleng Ta!

**Thleng Ta!** is a simple, reliable attendance and staff presence system designed for real-world teams, shops, cafes, and small businesses.

It focuses on **showing up**, **being present**, and **recording attendance without friction**.

---

## What Thleng Ta! Does

Thleng Ta! helps teams record attendance using:

- A **single QR code per store**
- Any phone with a **basic QR scanner**
- **No app installation** for employees

Employees simply scan, verify, and go.

---

## Why Thleng Ta! Exists

Most attendance systems:
- Force employees to install apps
- Require constant logins
- Break on low-end phones
- Are overkill for small teams

Thleng Ta! is built to be:
- Lightweight
- Fast
- Practical
- Reliable in real locations

If a phone can scan a QR, it can use Thleng Ta!.

---

## How It Works (Employee View)

1. Scan the store QR code  
2. Enter personal PIN  
3. Location is verified inside store radius  
4. Attendance is recorded instantly  

No accounts. No apps. No confusion.

---

## How It Works (Admin / Manager View)

Admins and managers can:

- Create stores
- Generate store QR codes
- Manage employees and PINs
- View attendance logs
- Adjust store location radius if needed

Everything is controlled from a simple web dashboard.

---

## Key Design Principles

Thleng Ta! is built on these ideas:

- **Simplicity beats features**
- **QR first, apps last**
- **Works on cheap phones**
- **Designed for physical locations**
- **Minimal UI, maximum clarity**

---

## Who Thleng Ta! Is For

- Cafes

- Teams with shared physical spaces

If your staff must *arrive at a place*, Thleng Ta! fits.

---

## What Thleng Ta! Is Not

- Not a GPS tracker
- Not a productivity monitor
- Not a surveillance tool

It records **presence**, not behavior.

---

## Philosophy

Attendance is not about control.
It is about **showing up**.

Thleng Ta! exists to answer one simple question clearly:

**“Have you arrived?”**
